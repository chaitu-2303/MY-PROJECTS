Our rent prediction model uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on extensive historical data. It typically achieves an accuracy rate of 85-90% for most urban areas, with predictions falling within 5-10% of actual market rates.

To list your property, you need to create an owner account, verify your identity, and then use our property listing form to add details about your property. You can upload photos, specify amenities, and set your preferred rent. Our system will also suggest an optimal rent based on our prediction model.

Our prediction model considers numerous factors including location, property size, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, amenities, furnishing status, property age, nearby facilities, and current market trends. Local economic indicators and seasonal variations are also factored into our predictions.

Basic rent predictions and property searches are free for all users. Property owners can list up to two properties for free, with a nominal fee for additional listings. We also offer premium services for landlords and property managers, including advanced analytics and featured listings.
