Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Our rent prediction model is built using advanced machine learning algorithms and trained on extensive real estate data. It typically provides predictions with an accuracy rate of 85-90% depending on the location and property type. We continuously update our model with the latest market data to ensure the highest possible accuracy.

To list your property, you need to register as an owner on our platform. After registration, you can access the owner dashboard and click on "Add Property." Fill in the required details about your property, upload high-quality images, set your rental price, and submit for review. Once approved, your property will be visible to potential tenants.

Our basic property listing service is free for property owners. We charge a small commission (5% of the first month's rent) only when a successful booking is made through our platform. For tenants, our service is completely free to use, including property searches and rent predictions.

You can schedule a property viewing directly through our platform. Simply browse available properties, select the one you're interested in, and click on the "Schedule Viewing" button. Choose your preferred date and time from the available slots, and submit your request. The property owner will receive your request and confirm the viewing appointment.
